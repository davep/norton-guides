 Is there a Clipper convention?

 For information on the CA-Technicon conferences held in several locations
 around the world, check with Client Care.  In the US, Technicon will
 be held in New Orleans in August 1996, and you can get information by
 calling 800-CA-INFO-96, which is 800-224-6369.

 There is a (US) West Coast Clipper Devcon hosted by the San Diego Clipper
 Users Group.  Contact Mick Keily at 71760,1346 on Compuserve.
