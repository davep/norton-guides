DEC destination

        Logic   destination ← destination - 1

    DEC subtracts one from the operand. Note that DEC does not change
    the carry flag.

    
    Opcode      Format
    48 + rw     DEC  r16
    48 + rd     DEC  r32
    FE /1       DEC  r/m8
    FF /1       DEC  r/m16
    FF /1       DEC  r/m32
