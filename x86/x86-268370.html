FINCSTP

        Logic   TOP ← (TOP + 1) AND 07h

    FINCSTP adds one to the three-bit TOP field of the FPU status word.
    In effect, FINCSTP rotates the stack; it is not equivalent to
    popping a register from the stack. If TOP is 7, this instruction
    makes it 0.


    Opcode      Format
    D9 F7       FINCSTP
