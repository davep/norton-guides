0040h:0095h           Drive 1 Current Track

    Length: 1 byte

    This byte is reserved for the PC, PCjr, PC XT dated 11/8/82, and
    the PC Convertible. For all others, this byte contains the current
    cylinder for floppy diskette drive 1.
