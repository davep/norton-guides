        CHANGE_STORE_TO_ASSIGNMENT

    Default: YES

    Options: YES, NO

    When set to YES, results in:

    STORE 'Hello' + " World" to y, z, x
    STORE x to y

    being processed into:

    y := z := x := 'Hello' + " World"
