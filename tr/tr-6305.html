 ALLTRIM(<expC>)
 Removes blank spaces from both ends of a string.
 Returns <expC> with leading and trailing blanks removed.

 Same as LTRIM( TRIM( <expC> ) ).

 ALLTRIM("      X Y Z       ") returns "X Y Z"
