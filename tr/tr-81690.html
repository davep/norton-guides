 POWER(<N number>, <N power>)
 Raises a number to a power.
 Returns <expN> of <number> raised to the power of <power>.

 <number> being raised to <power>.

 POWER(  2, 3 ) returns 8
 POWER( 10, 6 ) returns 10,000,000
