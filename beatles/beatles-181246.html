How Do You Do It - The Beatles
(This is the unreleased [by The Beatles] song that George Martin wanted
them to record as their second single.  They wanted to do their own song.
So, he told them to come up with a better song than this one, and they
could.  They presented him with a reworked version of "Please Please Me"
[which they had tried before], and they recorded that.  Afterwards,
George Martin told them that they had recorded their first number one
record.  He was proven right in January, 1963.)

If I knew how you do it to me, I'd do it to you.

Wish I knew how you do it to me, but I haven't a clue.
You give me a feeling in my heart (ooh la la)
Like an arrow passing through.
I suppose that you think you're very smart,
But won't you please tell me how do you do it?
