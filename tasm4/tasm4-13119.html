ALIGN            Aligns on specified boundary                       Directive

  ALIGN boundary

    Rounds up the location counter to a power-of-two address boundary (2,
    4, 8, ...).

    You can't reliably align to a boundary that is more strict than the
    segment alignment in which the ALIGN directive appears.

    Example:    ALIGN   4   ; Align to DWORD boundary for 386+
                BigNum  DD  12345678
