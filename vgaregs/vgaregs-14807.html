Vertical total         index 06h  CRTC  protected

Layout  b7-b0  VT   Vertical total

        The low-order eight bits of the value that determines the
        total number of vertical scan lines minus two (including
        blanked and border characters). A ninth and tenth bit are
        found in the Overflow register.
