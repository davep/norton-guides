 European Users Support
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

 Access to support services for registered users:

        i) Unlimited access to our BBS, The Dark Knight
       ii) Email via our Internet address
      iii) 30 days telephone support

 These methods of support come into effect from the date that we receive your
 registration details.

 European users are those that bought MrDebug from a legitiamte European
 source. If you do not have a serial number on the Memory/Info window then
 you are not a European user of MrDebug nd will have to contact your supplier
 for support services or, contact the US distributor.

 Note: Registration is required for support services.

 The preferred method of contacting Dark Black Software Limited the authors,
 is via electronic mail, on the Dark Knight Returns BBS.


 The Dark Knight Returns BBS

 The authors of MrDebug have their own bulletin board that is accessed by the
 authors every few hours. Our bulletin board contains many clipper related
 file and message areas of interest to CA-Clipper developers, including
 patches, fixes, example code, and experts who read and reply to messages if
 you need help.

 To access The Dark Knight Returns BBS set your communications software to
 2400-21600 baud with No parity, 8 data bits and 1 stop bit.

          The Dark Knight Returns BBS: (+44) (0)1480 471465

 We offer unlimited support for MrDebug on our BBS. There are no charges for
 using our BBS, we only ask that you upload files from time to time that you
 think may be of interest to other users.

 You can change to the MrDebug Support Files area by using the C command to
 list the file areas in the main area of our BBS. Select the number next to
 the MrDebug Support area. This should change you to the MrDebug Support
 files area where you may download the latest patch, free of charge.

 You can browse our message areas using the A command to list and change
 message areas or you can save time and an increased phone bill by using an
 offline mail reader to read and reply to messages offline. At the main menu,
 the MP command will give you access to our .QWK compatible offline mail
 reader.

 Once you are connected to our BBS, at the Main Menu use the 'M' command to
 leave a message to SysOp  with your MrDebug serial number - this way we will
 know who you are and be able to upgrade you with special privileges.


 The Internet

 We finally took the plunge and jumped back into the Dark Ages and joined an
 Internet provider. If you have an Internet account you can email us for
 support on MrDebug at the following address:

       MrDebug@darkblak.demon.co.uk

 Faxing us - our staff are all capable of reading

 Most of our staff have some understanding of reading and writing (few have
 yet to suss the art of spelling!). You may fax information through to our
 support staff on (+44) (0)1480 471465 - yes it is the same as our BBS -
 that's technological breakthroughs for ya!

 If you are going to fax us something, please make sure that you explain
 everything clearly. If you are faxing a problem through then please explain
 the problem, perhaps supply a small sample snippet of code that will help to
 reproduce the problem. A link file is always a good thing to send us, as
 this helps us to understand what is going on inside your program.


 Compuserve

 You mail email us from Compuserve by sending electronic mail to
 INTERNET:MrDebug@darkblak.demon.co.uk this will enable you to send email to
 us via the Internet. This will get a reply from us within 48 hours.


 Telephone

 As a last resort, you may contact us by telephone. It would also help us out
 if you have the following information ready for the ogre on our end of the
 telephone line:

        1. your MrDebug serial number
        2. the MrDebug version number (Shift-Alt-M from within MrDebug)
        3. the version of Clipper that you are using (Shift-Alt-M from within
           MrDebug)
        4. the name of your linker that you are using
        5. and some general information about the PC setup

 European Users can telephone Technical Support from 10.00am until 4.00pm GMT
 (Greenwich Mean Time). Call (+44) (0)1480 403104

 It is recommended that you not call in the morning. As we don't employ human
 beings in the mornings. The later in the morning that you call, the more
 coffee we will have had and the more awake we will be and hence the more
 effective we will be at helping you out.
