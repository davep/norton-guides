 Changing the colour scheme
 Everyone has their own colour preferences
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

 Okay so you don't like our colour scheme. We get the hint! The colours used
 by MrDebug are highly configurable and of course highly likely to be abused!

 You may change the colours used by MrDebug through the options menu of
 MrDebug.

 Under the Display Setup sub-menu is an item called Colours. This allows you
 to configure each and every colour that MrDebug uses as part of it's makeup.

 Choosing the Colour option bring up a colour selection box listing name of
 the colour section that may be changed. You may scroll up and down this list
 to reveal further colour items that you may change. As you scroll up and
 down the list, a representation of the part of MrDebug that will be effected
 by the colour change will appear on the right hand side of the screen.

 To change a colour simply press ◄──┛ on the item in the list. A colour
 selection box will appear. This will allow you to 'try before you buy' the
 colour changes.
