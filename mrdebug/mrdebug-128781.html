 Main Menu
 Using the mouse with the main menu.
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

 To select an item from the main menu using the mouse, move the mouse cursor
 to the top of the screen, click and hold down the left mouse button. Whilst
 you keep the mouse button down, the menu will appear. Release the mouse
 button and the menu will disappear.

 Whilst you have the left hand mouse button held down, you may move the mouse
 left and right. If the mouse is positioned on the very top line, then each
 of the drop down menus will appear as you move the mouse cursor.

 You may select an item from a menu by simply moving th mouse cursor down
 over a menu item and releasing the mouse button. As you move up and down the
 menu items a highlight bar will appear over the currently selected menu
 item.

 If you do not wish to select and item, then move the mouse until the mouse
 is no longer on a menu, or a highlight bar no longer appears over a menu
 item, and simply let go of the mouse button.
