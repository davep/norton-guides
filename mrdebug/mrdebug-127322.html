 Re-sizing a window with a mouse
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 To re-size a window click and hold down the left mouse button on the bottom
 right hand corner of the window. You may now drag the window to either
 expand or reduce it's size.

 Remember to keep any changes you have made to the windows, you must use the
 save settings item contained in the options menu - or have auto-save on.

 When windows contain more information than can be contained within the
 windows, scroll bars will allow you to change scroll up, down, left or
 right. You can click either on the arrows of the scroll bars to move a
 single item at a time, or on the actual scroll bars and drag them to move
 heaps of items at a time.
