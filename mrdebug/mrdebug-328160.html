 Navigating your way around MrDebug
 How to work your way around MrDebug
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

 Most of the items that you'll need to start of with can be found under the
 pull down menus. You may activate the pull down menus by tapping the <Alt>
 key. This will bring up the menu bar. You may then select a specific pull
 down menu by typing the letter that stands out from the others in the menu
 name.

 A pull down menu should appear from the menu bar with a list of items to
 choose from. On each of the menu items there is a key letter that stand out
 from the other letters. Type this letter and it will activate the option or
 toggle associated with the menu item.

 To select a specific menu you may hold down <ALT> and press the menu
 hot-key. For example the Options pull-down menu is <Alt><O>.

 Initially you will be using the menus for most things, but as you become
 more familiar with MrDebug you will learn that most of the important
 features of MrDebug have keys assigned to them to save you time.
