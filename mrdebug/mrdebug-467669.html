 Profile Window
 Toggles the Profile Window on or off.
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

 This option toggles whether the Profiler window is displayed or not.

 Toggling this option to on displays the profiler window, you may then move
 and re-size it to suit your needs.

 Toggling this option to off, hides the Profiler window and does not destroy
 or lose and information in the profiler.

 The hot-key combination for this item is <Shift><Alt><V>.
