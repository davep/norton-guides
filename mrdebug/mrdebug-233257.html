 About MrDebug
 Overview of MrDebug
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

 MrDebug is an advanced debugger for CA-Clipper, version 5.2c or higher that
 is linked into your programs as a library file.

 Since MrDebug is a library only file, MrDebug can be used in either
 real-mode or protected mode, with 286-DOS extenders, 386-DOS extenders and
 beyond.

 MrDebug has been tested, and is compatible with Blinker, Causeway, ClipXMS,
 Exospace and .RTLink.

 Warning: use of MrDebug can prove addictive. We found that our beta testers
 became 'hooked' on MrDebug. When we denied them access to MrDebug we nearly
 had a mutiny on our hands.


 Meet the Family.

 MrDebug for Windows is our latest creation. Imagine a windows based
 debugger for Clip-4-Win or FiveWin with all the features of MrDebug.
