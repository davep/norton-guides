 Maximum Usage of Screen Area
 MrDebug takes advantage of your text mode
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

 To really get swinging with MrDebug we recommend that you locate the drivers
 that came with your video card and set the text mode to it's highest size
 i.e. 132 columns x 60 rows, or 132x50, or even 132x43.

 Run your application with MrDebug in it and, MrDebug will take advantage of
 this huge screen area that you now have available. The application window
 will, by default be in it's own 80x25 window.

 This leaves you heaps of space for your debugging tools, around the
 application window.
