 Should I be getting duplicate message warnings from my linker?
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

 When linking MrDebug you may find that your linker gives you some warning
 messages about duplicate symbol re-definitions.

 These should not give you cause for alarm as we are simply replacing parts
 of CLD held in CLIPPER.LIB, with modules of the same name held in
 MrDebug.LIB

 The following are examples of the typical duplicate messages that you may
 come across, in this example we have used Blinker as an example for our
 output:

   BLINKER : 1004 : _MRDERSY.OBJ(_MRDERSY) : 'ERRORSYS' is duplicated in
                    CLIPPER.LIB(ERRORSYS)
   BLINKER : 1004 : MRDEBUG.LIB(_MRDDUM) : '__DBGMENU' is duplicated in
                    CLIPPER.LIB(_DBGMENU)
   BLINKER : 1004 : MRDEBUG.LIB(_MRDCLIP) : '__DBGCLR' is duplicated in
                    CLIPPER.LIB(_DBGMENU)
   BLINKER : 1004 : MRDEBUG.LIB(_MRDDUM) : '__DBGERROR' is duplicated in
                    CLIPPER.LIB(_DBGINSP)
   BLINKER : 1004 : MRDEBUG.LIB(_MRDDBFL) : '__DBFLIST' is duplicated in
                    CLIPPER.LIB(_DBFLIST)
   BLINKER : 1004 : MRDEBUG.LIB(_MRDDUM) : '__DBGHELP' is duplicated in
                    CLIPPER.LIB(_DBGHELP)
