Int 62 Fn 004F  u - Fgdriver V4.02 - "fg_getview" - Get Viewport Extreme   [V]

   AX = 004Fh
   ES:BX -> variable pointer record (see #3158)

Return: indicated variables updated

Note:  the FGDRIVER functions are rearranged with each major release, but
     their parameters do not change (see #3145 at AX=0000h)

See Also: AX=00B5h

Format of FGDRIVER GETVIEW variable pointer record:
