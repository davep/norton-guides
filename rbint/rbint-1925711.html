Bitfields for Intel 82371FB/82371SB SMI Control Register:
Bit(s) Description (Table 0920)
 7-5   reserved

   01 disabled (frozen)
   10 one PCICLK
   11 one millisecond

   =1 enable Clock Scale bytes in PCI configuration space
 1 STPCLK# signal enable
   =1 assert STPCLK# on read from PORT 00B2h
