How to register.


If you like the library and you want to register,
just fill out the registration form supplied in REGISTER.FRM, print it and
send it to :

                    Ferns Paanakker
                    Looikolkstraat 2
                    7451 EP Holten
                    The Netherlands.


And please don't forget to enclose the registration and shipping and handling
fee too.

By the way, the fee is :

                               HOLLAND   ELSEWHERE
    registration               Fl 50,-    $ 45,-
    shipping and handling      Fl  5,-    $ 10,-

For more details see REGISTER.FRM.

After registration you will :

 - receive the registered library (so no shareware notice anymore);
 - receive the complete Norton Guide;
 - receive the Dutch and American Norton Guide;
 - receive notification of new major versions;
 - get support;
 - be legalized.
