Because of the number of Norton Guide tools I've published, on occasion I get asked if I have a copy of a particular Norton Guide file. While there are a couple of very large collections of guides out there on the web, it looks like they're starting to disappear; at least one that was build using ng2html seems to have gone offline.

With this in mind, I'm going to populate this little site with all the guides I have that I have a licence to publish in public. Let me repeat and make clear that part: The only guides that are published here are guides that permit distribution and republishing.

If there is a particular guide you are after, and it isn't listed here, there's little point in contacting me and asking for a copy; as much as I'd love to help I would prefer not to distribute files I don't have a licence to distribute.
