Welcome to Expert Guide.

If you are reading this the chances are that you've just installed Expert
Guide. What you are reading is a default guide that was installed for you.
Press 'v' if you want to see where it was installed (look at the status line
at the bottom of the terminal).

Press '?' for keyboard usage help.

`man eg' will allow you to read the Expert Guide man page.

Press 'd' for a directory of your guides (by default Expert Guide looks in
/usr/share/norton-guides and /usr/local/share/norton-guides for guides).
