Int 10 Fn 0070  - Video - Everex Micro Enhancer Ega/viewpoint Vga - Exten  [V]

   AX = 0070h
   BL = mode (see #0010)
Desc:  specify a proprietary display mode on the Everex Micro Enhancer or
     Viewpoint video adapters

See Also: AH=00h,AX=6F05h,AX=7000h/BX=0004h,AH=FFh"GO32"
