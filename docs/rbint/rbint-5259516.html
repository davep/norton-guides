Int 62 Fn 0038  u - Fgdriver V4.02 - "fg_freepage" - Free Virtual Or Logi  [V]

   AX = 0038h
   BX = page number (0000h-003Fh)

Return: AX = status (0000h,0001h,0007h,0009h) (see #3144)

Note:  the FGDRIVER functions are rearranged with each major release, but
     their parameters do not change (see #3145 at AX=0000h)

See Also: AX=0000h,AX=0001h,AX=0002h,AX=0003h
