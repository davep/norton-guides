Int 15 Fn 4DD4  - Hp 95lx/100lx/200lx - Installation Check                 [b]

   AX = 4DD4h

       0101h HP 95LX
       0102h HP 100LX/200LX
       DH = ???
       02h HP 200LX 2MB BIOS 1.01 A D german
       DL = ???
       00h HP 95LX
       01h HP 200LX 2MB BIOS 1.01 A D german
