/mu       Converts symbols to uppercase

          The /mu option tells Turbo Assembler to ignore the case of all
          symbols.

          By default, Turbo Assembler specifies that any lowercase letters
          in symbols will be converted to uppercase unless you change it
          by using the /ml directive.
