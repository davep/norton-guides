Need structure member name

In Ideal mode, the period (.) structure member operator was followed by
something that was not a structure member name. For example,

      IDEAL
      STRUC DEMO
            DB   ?
      ENDS
      COUNT      DW 0
      mov ax,[(DEMO bx).]

You must always follow the period operator with the name of a member in
the structure to its left.
