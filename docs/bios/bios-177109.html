Int 16h, 04h            Adjust Keyboard Click               PCjr, Convertible

    Turns keyboard click on or off. This service is available only on
    the PCjr and the PC Convertible.

        Entry   AH = 04h
                AL = Keyboard click
                     | 00h  off
                     | 01h  on

        Return  Nothing
