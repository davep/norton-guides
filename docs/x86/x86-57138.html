INC             Increment by 1                       Flags: O D I T S Z A P C
                                                            * - - - * * * * -
INC destination

        Logic   destination ← destination + 1

    INC adds one to the operand. Note that INC does not change the
    carry flag.

    
    Opcode      Format
    40 + rw     INC  r16
    40 + rd     INC  r32
    FE /0       INC  r/m8
    FF /0       INC  r/m16
    FF /0       INC  r/m32
