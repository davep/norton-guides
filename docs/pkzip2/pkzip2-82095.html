  Minimum DOS Requirements for SPANNING & FORMATTING

  For PKZIP 2 to span multiple disks with a .ZIP file, DOS 3.0 or greater is
  required.

  To be able to format diskettes on-the-fly, DOS 3.2 or greater is required.
