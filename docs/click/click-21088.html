        CLEAN_UP_INCLUDES

    Default: YES

    This Determines if #includes will be beautified. Beautification
    consists of switching the #define to lower case, converting the
    delimiters to " and upper casing the file name.

    #INCLUDE 'somefile.CH'
    #include "nextFILE.CH"

    becomes

    #include "SOMEFILE.CH"
    #include "NEXTFILE.CH"
