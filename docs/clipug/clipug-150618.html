Directions to Meeting: 
Directions to the Monthly Technical Meetings at the Crystal City Marriott    
(not the Gateway Marriott)                                                   
                                                                             
By Metro:                                                                    
   - Take the Blue or Yellow Line to Crystal City                            
   - Go up the first escalator                                               
   - Walk left through the door.                                             
   - Follow signs in tunnel to Marriott elevator banks.                      
                                                                             
From the South:                                                              
   - Take I-395 north to the Crystal City exit.                              
   - Hayes Street turns into 18th Street.                                    
   - Straight ahead to the Crystal City Marriott.                            
                                                                             
From the West and North (American Legion Bridge):                            
   - Take I-495 (the Beltway) south.                                         
   - Take I-66 East to the Pentagon (Route 110) exit.                        
   - Take the Crystal City (US 1 South) exit.                                
   - Turn left onto 20th Street.                                             
                                                                             
From downtown Washington (14th Street Bridge) or G.W. Parkway:               
   - Take I-395 South to the Crystal City (US 1 South) LEFT exit.            
   - Turn left onto 20th Street.                                             
                                                                             
There is plenty of free parking on 18th Street, 20th Street, and Crystal     
Drive.  All meters are free after 6pm.
